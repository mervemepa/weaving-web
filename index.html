Weaving with Text - Version 1.0.0

The project 'anonymousText.tie' is the output of a critical investigation on potentials of integrating traditional weaving techniques with social and technical developments that led to current gender situations. Just as the technological development itself can be perceived as a "feminization" of culture that will lead to an increasing dissolution of gender, the art of weaving can also lead to a subversion of gender roles. For this reason for centuries, weavers have employed a customized textual weaving algorithm, called ‘name drafting’, to encode their personal stories and experiences on rugs and textiles. Here at the middle draft displays the weaving pattern with the logic of punch cards that generates the name drafting algorithm by your personal words or sentences on threads and pedals. The project attempts to archive daily or periodic concerns and situations, as women do through history by weaving, and reverse it through today’s communication tools. This approach not only empowers expression itself through new forms of storytelling, but also contributes to a larger discourse on gender equality and social justice, ultimately creating new possibilities for the future...
